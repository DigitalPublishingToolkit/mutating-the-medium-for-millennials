About Even Up

One of the big Dutch publishers WPG Uitgevers posed the question how to create a commercially and journalistically sustainable platform, inspired by their content and targeted at millennials. Thus, we built a prototype of a website on the topic of mindstyle, which is the core topic for three of WPG’s magazines: Happinez, Yoga and Psychologie. Researching several aspects of the development for a digital platform, we created a new brand with a visual identity and guidelines for its content. Our project resulted in a platform called ‘Even Up’, based on the theme of ‘balance’.
